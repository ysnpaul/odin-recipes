Lasagna Recipe

This is the recipe for a delicious Lasagna. Anyone can do it!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb. lasagna noodles
  • 1 tsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 2 lb. ground beef
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp. dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Cook ground beef in medium heat
  3. combine Ingredients in large pot
  4. stick it in the oven
